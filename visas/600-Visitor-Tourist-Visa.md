# Subclass 600 - Visitor/Tourist Visa

## Section 1: Executive Overview

The Visitor visa (subclass 600) is Australia's most popular tourist visa, designed for individuals who wish to visit Australia temporarily for tourism, business visitor activities, or to visit family and friends. This visa offers flexibility with multiple streams catering to different travel purposes.

**Key Features:**
- **Duration:** 3, 6, or 12 months depending on the stream and circumstances
- **Cost:** Starting from AUD $190 (base application charge)
- **Processing:** Typically 14-45 days for most applications
- **Entry:** Single or multiple entries depending on grant conditions

**Available Streams:**
1. **Tourist Stream (Apply Outside Australia)** - For tourism, visiting family/friends, or cruises
2. **Tourist Stream (Apply In Australia)** - For extending stays for tourism purposes
3. **Sponsored Family Stream** - For those sponsored by family members in Australia
4. **Business Visitor Stream** - For short-term business activities
5. **Approved Destination Status Stream** - For Chinese citizens on organized tours
6. **Frequent Traveller Stream** - Multiple-entry visa for eligible passport holders

## Section 2: Eligibility Requirements

### General Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Subclass 600 visa, you must:

1. **Genuine Temporary Entrant (GTE)**
   - Demonstrate genuine intention to visit Australia temporarily
   - Show strong ties to your home country (employment, family, assets)
   - Provide evidence of reasons to return home

2. **Financial Capacity**
   - Have sufficient funds to support yourself during your stay
   - Show evidence of funds through bank statements, pay slips, or sponsorship
   - Credit card limits and traveler's cheques may also be considered

3. **Health Requirements**
   - Meet Australia's health criteria
   - Undergo medical examinations if required
   - Hold adequate health insurance (recommended, as Medicare coverage is limited)

4. **Character Requirements**
   - No serious criminal history
   - Provide police certificates if requested
   - Declare any criminal convictions

5. **Other Requirements**
   - Have a valid passport
   - Not have a condition 8503 (No Further Stay) on a current visa
   - Be outside Australia when applying (for offshore streams)

### Stream-Specific Requirements

**Sponsored Family Stream:**
- Must have an eligible family member sponsor in Australia
- Sponsor may need to provide a bond (AUD $5,000-$15,000)
- Sponsor must be settled Australian citizen, PR holder, or eligible NZ citizen

**Business Visitor Stream:**
- Business-related purpose of visit
- Evidence of business activities (meetings, conferences, negotiations)
- May require letter of invitation from Australian business

## Section 3: Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Determine the Right Stream
Review the available streams and select the one that matches your purpose of visit.

### Step 2: Create an ImmiAccount
- Register at https://online.immi.gov.au
- Verify your email address
- Complete your profile information

### Step 3: Complete the Online Application
- Select "New Application"
- Choose "Visitor" → "Visitor (600)"
- Answer all questions accurately and completely
- Declare any health or character issues

### Step 4: Upload Supporting Documents
- Identity documents (passport bio-page)
- Financial evidence
- Purpose of visit documentation
- Additional stream-specific documents

### Step 5: Pay the Application Fee
- Base charge: AUD $190 (offshore)
- Additional applicant charges apply for family members
- Payment via credit/debit card through ImmiAccount

### Step 6: Biometrics Collection (if required)
- Some nationalities must provide biometrics
- Attend appointment at Australian Visa Application Centre (AVAC)
- Book appointment after receiving request

### Step 7: Health Examinations (if required)
- Complete through panel physicians
- Results sent directly to Department of Home Affairs

### Step 8: Wait for Decision
- Monitor application status in ImmiAccount
- Respond promptly to any requests for additional information

### Step 9: Receive Visa Outcome
- Notification via email
- Visa grant details in ImmiAccount
- Check visa conditions carefully

## Section 4: Document Checklist

### Essential Documents (All Streams)

**Identity Documents:**
- [ ] Passport bio-data page (valid for at least 6 months)
- [ ] Recent passport-sized photograph (45mm x 35mm)
- [ ] National ID card (if applicable)
- [ ] Birth certificate (for minors)

**Financial Evidence:**
- [ ] Bank statements (last 3-6 months)
- [ ] Pay slips or employment letter
- [ ] Tax returns (if self-employed)
- [ ] Credit card statements (showing limit)
- [ ] Evidence of assets (property, investments)

**Purpose of Visit:**
- [ ] Travel itinerary
- [ ] Flight bookings (not required before application)
- [ ] Hotel reservations or accommodation details
- [ ] Letter explaining purpose of visit

### Stream-Specific Documents

**Tourist Stream:**
- [ ] Evidence of employment/studies in home country
- [ ] Proof of ties to home country
- [ ] Previous travel history documentation

**Sponsored Family Stream:**
- [ ] Form 1149 (Application for Sponsorship)
- [ ] Sponsor's proof of status in Australia
- [ ] Evidence of relationship to sponsor
- [ ] Sponsor's financial documents
- [ ] Form 1257 (Undertaking declaration, for minors)

**Business Visitor Stream:**
- [ ] Letter from employer confirming business purpose
- [ ] Invitation letter from Australian company
- [ ] Evidence of business activities (conference registration, meeting schedules)
- [ ] Business registration documents

**Minors Under 18:**
- [ ] Birth certificate showing both parents' names
- [ ] Parental consent (Form 1229 or statutory declaration)
- [ ] Copy of parents' passports or ID
- [ ] Evidence of legal guardianship (if applicable)

## Section 8: Common Mistakes & Tips

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Insufficient Financial Evidence**
   - Mistake: Providing only one bank statement
   - Solution: Submit 3-6 months of statements showing consistent funds

2. **Weak GTE Statement**
   - Mistake: Generic or copy-paste statements
   - Solution: Personalize your statement with specific details about ties to home country

3. **Incomplete Documentation**
   - Mistake: Missing required documents
   - Solution: Use the document checklist; submit all requested items

4. **Incorrect Stream Selection**
   - Mistake: Applying for wrong stream
   - Solution: Carefully review stream requirements before applying

5. **Inconsistent Information**
   - Mistake: Contradictory information across documents
   - Solution: Cross-check all details before submission

### Expert Tips for Success

**Before Applying:**
- Apply at least 2-3 months before intended travel
- Ensure passport has at least 6 months validity
- Check if you need biometrics from your country

**During Application:**
- Be honest and transparent
- Provide clear, high-quality document scans
- Explain any gaps or unusual circumstances

**After Submission:**
- Check ImmiAccount regularly
- Respond to requests within specified timeframes
- Notify DHA of any changes in circumstances

### Red Flags to Avoid:
- Previous visa overstays or breaches
- Criminal history (undeclared)
- Insufficient ties to home country
- Suspicious financial transactions
- Inconsistent travel history

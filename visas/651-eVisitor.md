# Subclass 651 - eVisitor Visa

## Section 1: Executive Overview

The eVisitor (subclass 651) is a free visa option designed specifically for European Union passport holders visiting Australia for tourism or business purposes. Similar to the ETA, it allows multiple entries over a 12-month period.

**Key Features:**
- **Cost:** FREE (no application fee)
- **Duration:** 12 months from grant date
- **Stay Period:** Up to 3 months per visit
- **Processing:** Usually within minutes to days
- **Entry:** Multiple entries permitted

**Important Note:**
The eVisitor visa is only available to citizens of European Union member states and select European countries. It cannot be extended while in Australia.

---

## Section 2: Eligibility Requirements

### Passport Requirements

You must hold a valid passport from one of these eligible countries:

| Country Category | Eligible Countries |
|------------------|-------------------|
| **European Union Members** | Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Ireland, Italy, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Netherlands, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden |
| **Other European** | Andorra, Iceland, Liechtenstein, Monaco, Norway, San Marino, Switzerland, United Kingdom (British Citizen), Vatican City |

**Not Eligible:**
- British National Overseas (BNO) passport holders
- British Dependent Territories Citizens
- British Overseas Citizens
- British Protected Persons
- British Subjects

### General Eligibility Criteria

1. **Genuine Temporary Entrant**
   - Genuine intention to visit temporarily
   - Tourism or business visitor purposes only
   - No work rights (beyond business visitor activities)

2. **Health Requirements**
   - Meet standard health criteria
   - No tuberculosis
   - Must not pose public health risk

3. **Character Requirements**
   - No serious criminal convictions (12 months+ sentence)
   - No visa cancellations
   - Must not be subject to exclusion period

4. **Other Conditions**
   - Must apply from outside Australia
   - Must be outside Australia when visa is granted
   - Must not hold any other visa at time of grant

### Ineligibility Factors

You cannot apply for an eVisitor if:
- You intend to work in Australia
- You intend to study for more than 3 months
- You have a criminal conviction totaling 12+ months
- You hold a non-eligible passport
- You are in Australia at time of application

## Section 3: Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Create an ImmiAccount
- Visit https://online.immi.gov.au
- Click "Create an Account"
- Enter email and create password
- Verify email address

### Step 2: Start New Application
- Log into ImmiAccount
- Select "New Application"
- Choose "Visitor" → "eVisitor (651)"

### Step 3: Complete Application Form
- Enter passport details
- Provide personal information
- Answer health questions
- Answer character questions
- Declare any criminal convictions

### Step 4: Upload Documents
- Passport bio-page scan
- Any additional requested documents

### Step 5: Submit Application
- Review all information
- Submit application (no fee required)
- Note application reference number

### Step 6: Wait for Decision
- Most processed within minutes to days
- Check email and ImmiAccount for updates

### Step 7: Receive Visa Grant
- Visa electronically linked to passport
- Notification via email
- Check visa conditions carefully

## Section 8: Common Mistakes & Tips

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Wrong Passport Type**
   - Mistake: BNO or other non-eligible British passports
   - Solution: Must hold British Citizen passport

2. **Applying While in Australia**
   - Mistake: Submitting application onshore
   - Solution: Must be outside Australia to apply

3. **Planning to Work**
   - Mistake: Intending to work on eVisitor
   - Solution: eVisitor does not permit work

4. **Incorrect Personal Details**
   - Mistake: Typos in passport details
   - Solution: Triple-check all information

5. **Passport Validity Issues**
   - Mistake: Passport expiring soon
   - Solution: Ensure 6+ months validity

### Expert Tips for Success

**Before Applying:**
- Verify passport eligibility
- Ensure passport is valid for intended stay
- Have digital copy of passport ready

**During Application:**
- Use accurate, consistent information
- Answer all questions honestly
- Save application progress regularly

**After Grant:**
- Note visa grant number
- Check visa conditions
- Print confirmation for travel

### eVisitor Conditions

**You CAN:**
- Visit Australia multiple times
- Stay up to 3 months per visit
- Engage in tourism activities
- Conduct business visitor activities
- Study for up to 3 months

**You CANNOT:**
- Work in Australia
- Stay longer than 3 months per visit
- Extend the eVisitor while in Australia
- Apply for another eVisitor while holding current one

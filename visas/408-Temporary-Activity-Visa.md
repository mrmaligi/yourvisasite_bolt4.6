# Subclass 408 - Temporary Activity Visa

## 1. Executive Overview

The **Temporary Activity Visa (Subclass 408)** allows individuals to come to Australia to participate in specific types of work or activities on a short-term, temporary basis. This visa covers multiple streams including special programs, government activities, research, and events.

**Key Features:**
- Duration: 3 months to 4 years (depending on stream)
- Multiple Streams Available
- Work Rights: Varies by stream
- Study Rights: Generally permitted
- Cost: From AUD $310 - $430

**Note:** The COVID-19 Pandemic Event visa stream was closed on 1 July 2024.

---

## 2. Eligibility Requirements

### General Requirements:
- **Sponsorship/Invitation:** Must be sponsored, nominated, or invited by an approved organisation
- **Health & Character:** Meet Australia's health and character requirements
- **Insurance:** Maintain adequate health insurance (OVHC) for entire stay
- **Financial Support:** Demonstrate sufficient funds for duration of stay
- **Genuine Intent:** Genuine intention to participate in specified activity

### Available Streams:

#### 1. **Australian Government Endorsed Events**
- For activities endorsed by Australian Government
- Duration: Up to 4 years
- Includes sporting events, cultural exchanges

#### 2. **Special Program**
- Youth exchange, cultural enrichment, community benefit programs
- School language assistants, gap year volunteering
- Duration: Up to 12 months
- **English Requirement:** Functional English (IELTS 4.5 or equivalent)

#### 3. **Research Activities**
- For researchers, academics, scientists
- Must be invited by Australian organisation
- Duration: Varies based on research project

#### 4. **Staff Exchange**
- Part of staff exchange with Australian organisation
- Duration: Up to 2 years
- Family members can be included

#### 5. **Religious Work**
- For religious workers
- Sponsored by religious institution
- Duration: Up to 2 years

#### 6. **Entertainment Activities**
- For performers, crew, production staff
- Duration varies by project

#### 7. **Sporting Activities**
- For athletes, coaches, officials
- Duration varies by event/contract

#### 8. **Superyacht Crew**
- For crew members of superyachts
- Specific requirements apply

## 3. Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Determine Eligible Stream
- Identify which 408 stream matches your activity
- Review specific requirements for that stream
- Confirm sponsorship/invitation availability

### Step 2: Secure Sponsorship/Invitation
- Obtain formal invitation from Australian organisation
- OR secure sponsorship from approved Temporary Activities Sponsor
- For short stays (≤3 months): Support test may apply instead

### Step 3: Gather Documents
- Passport and identity documents
- Letter of invitation or sponsorship evidence
- Evidence of qualifications/experience
- Health insurance confirmation
- Police clearances
- Evidence of financial capacity

### Step 4: Lodge Application
- Create ImmiAccount
- Complete online application form
- Upload all supporting documents
- Pay visa application charge

### Step 5: Health and Biometrics
- Complete health examinations if requested
- Provide biometrics if required

### Step 6: Wait for Decision
- Monitor ImmiAccount for updates
- Respond to any requests promptly

## 8. Common Mistakes & Tips

### Common Mistakes:
1. **Wrong Stream Selection** - Choose the correct stream for your activity
2. **Insufficient Documentation** - Provide complete evidence of activity/engagement
3. **Missing Insurance** - OVHC required for entire stay
4. **Incomplete Invitation Letters** - Must include all required details
5. **Not Meeting Support Test** - For stays ≤3 months without sponsorship
6. **Working Outside Approved Activity** - Must stick to visa conditions
7. **Incorrect Remuneration Evidence** - Must meet Australian minimum standards

### Pro Tips:
1. **Start Early** - Some streams require extensive preparation
2. **Organisation Accreditation** - Ensure inviting organisation is reputable
3. **Clear Activity Description** - Provide detailed explanation of activities
4. **Check Stream Requirements** - Each stream has unique criteria
5. **Maintain Conditions** - Understand and comply with all visa conditions
6. **Keep Records** - Document all activities while in Australia

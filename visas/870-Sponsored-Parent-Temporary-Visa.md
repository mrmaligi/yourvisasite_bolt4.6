# Subclass 870 - Sponsored Parent (Temporary) Visa

## Section 1: Executive Overview

**Visa Type:** Temporary Parent Visa  
**Purpose:** Allows parents of Australian citizens/permanent residents to visit for extended periods (3-5 years)  
**Duration:** 3 or 5 years (renewable once for up to 10 years total)  
**Cost:** $1,000 (3 years) or $5,000 (5 years) - visa application charge  
**Processing Time:** 4-6 months

### What is the Subclass 870 Visa?

The Subclass 870 Sponsored Parent (Temporary) Visa is a **long-term temporary visa** that allows parents of Australian citizens, permanent residents, or eligible New Zealand citizens to stay in Australia for **extended periods** (3 or 5 years). Unlike visitor visas, this allows parents to stay without needing to leave regularly.

### Key Benefits

- **Extended stay** - 3 or 5 years without leaving
- **Multiple entries** - Travel to and from Australia
- **Work rights** - Can work (unlike some other parent visas)
- **Study permitted** - Can study for up to 3 months
- **No balance of family test** - Unlike permanent parent visas
- **Faster processing** - 4-6 months vs 30+ years for permanent parent visas
- **No Assurance of Support** - No AoS bond required

### Important Limitations

- **Temporary only** - No pathway to permanent residency
- **Sponsorship required** - Child must be approved as sponsor first
- **No Medicare access** - Must have private health insurance
- **High cost** - $1,000-$5,000 depending on duration
- **Sponsorship cap** - Limited number of sponsorships per year
- **Must apply offshore** - Must be outside Australia when applying

### Who Is This Visa For?

- Parents who want extended time with children in Australia
- Those who don't qualify for permanent parent visas
- Parents who don't meet balance of family test
- Those wanting faster alternative to 30-year parent visa queue
- Parents visiting for childcare support

## Section 2: Eligibility Requirements

### For the Parent (Applicant)

#### 1. Relationship to Sponsor
- Must be **biological, adoptive, or step-parent** of sponsor
- Step-parent relationship must be current
- Relationship must be genuine

#### 2. Genuine Temporary Entrant
- Must demonstrate **genuine intention to stay temporarily**
- Must have compelling reasons to return home
- Must show ties to home country

#### 3. Health Insurance
- Must maintain **adequate health insurance** for entire stay
- Must arrange before visa grant
- Coverage must meet DHA requirements

#### 4. Health and Character
- Must meet Australia's health standards
- Must meet character requirements
- Provide police clearances if requested

#### 5. Financial Capacity
- Must demonstrate ability to support yourself
- Or evidence of sponsor support
- Or combination of both

#### 6. Location
- Must be **outside Australia** when applying
- Must be outside Australia when visa is granted
- Can enter Australia after grant

### For the Sponsor (Child)

#### 1. Eligibility
- Must be **Australian citizen, permanent resident, or eligible NZ citizen**
- Must be 18 years or older
- Must be settled in Australia (usually 2+ years)

#### 2. Income Requirement
- Must meet **minimum household income** threshold
- Current threshold approximately **$83,454.80** (taxable income)
- Can include partner's income
- Can include other household members' income

#### 3. Sponsorship Approval
- Must apply for and be **approved as a sponsor** first
- Must pay sponsorship fee ($420)
- Approval valid for 6 months
- Limited to 2 sponsorships per year (per household)

#### 4. Sponsorship Obligations
- Provide support and accommodation
- Ensure parent complies with visa conditions
- Accept financial responsibility if parent defaults

### Additional Requirements

#### No Outstanding Debts
- Parent must have no debts to Australian government
- Or must have arranged repayment

#### Previous Visa Compliance
- Must have complied with previous Australian visas
- No visa cancellations or refusals (may affect application)

## Section 3: Step-by-Step Application Process

### Step 1: Confirm Eligibility (Week 1)
- Verify child is Australian citizen/permanent resident
- Check child meets income requirements
- Confirm parent meets health/character requirements
- Ensure parent is outside Australia

### Step 2: Child Applies for Sponsorship (Weeks 1-2)
- Child lodges sponsorship application
- Pay sponsorship fee ($420)
- Provide income evidence
- Wait for sponsorship approval (usually 4-8 weeks)

### Step 3: Parent Arranges Health Insurance (Weeks 2-4)
- Purchase Overseas Visitor Health Cover (OVHC)
- Ensure coverage meets requirements
- Coverage must be for full visa duration
- Keep policy documentation

### Step 4: Gather Parent Documents (Weeks 3-4)
- Identity documents
- Relationship evidence to sponsor
- Financial evidence
- Health and character documents

### Step 5: Create ImmiAccount (Week 4)
- Parent creates ImmiAccount
- Prepare for visa application

### Step 6: Parent Lodges Visa Application (Week 4-6)
- Wait for sponsorship approval
- Complete online visa application
- Pay visa application charge:
  - $1,000 for 3 years, OR
  - $5,000 for 5 years
- Upload all documents
- Submit application from outside Australia

### Step 7: Health Examinations (Weeks 6-10)
- Complete health examinations if requested
- Book through Bupa Medical Visa Services

### Step 8: Wait for Processing (Months 4-6)
- Processing typically 4-6 months
- Monitor ImmiAccount for updates
- Respond promptly to any requests

### Step 9: Visa Grant
- Receive grant notification
- Check visa conditions
- Make travel arrangements
- Enter Australia

## Section 7: Key Considerations

### Visa Conditions

The Subclass 870 visa includes:
- **Condition 8101:** Can work (limited work rights)
- **Condition 8201:** Maximum 3 months study
- **Condition 8501:** Maintain adequate health insurance
- **Condition 8531:** Must leave Australia before visa expires
- **Condition 8503:** No further stay (cannot extend or change visa onshore)

### No Further Stay Condition (8503)

**Important:** This visa includes a "No Further Stay" condition:
- Cannot apply for most other visas while in Australia
- Cannot extend this visa while in Australia
- Must leave Australia when visa expires
- Can apply for new 870 visa from outside Australia

### Medicare Access

**Subclass 870 holders CANNOT access Medicare:**
- Must have private health insurance (OVHC)
- Must pay for all medical treatment
- Cannot enroll in Medicare
- Health insurance is mandatory

### Renewal Options

- Can apply for **second 870 visa** after first expires
- Must apply from outside Australia
- Maximum stay: 10 years total
- Same sponsorship and eligibility requirements apply
- Second visa costs $5,000 (5 years only)

### Comparison with Permanent Parent Visas

| Feature | Subclass 870 | Permanent Parent Visas (103/143) |
|---------|--------------|----------------------------------|
| Processing Time | 4-6 months | 12-30+ years |
| Cost | $1,000-$5,000 | $4,000-$50,000+ |
| Permanent Residency | No | Yes |
| Medicare | No | Yes (after grant) |
| Balance of Family Test | No | Yes |
| Work Rights | Yes | Yes (after grant) |
| Pathway to Citizenship | No | Yes |

### Sponsorship Caps

- **2 sponsorships per household per year**
- Both parents count as 2 sponsorships
- Caps reset each financial year (July 1)
- Apply early in financial year if possible

## Section 8: Common Mistakes & How to Avoid Them

### Mistake 1: Insufficient Income Evidence
**Problem:** Sponsor doesn't meet income requirements.
**Solution:**
- Check current income threshold ($83,454.80+)
- Include partner's income if eligible
- Provide 2 years of tax evidence
- Consider combining household income

### Mistake 2: Weak Relationship Evidence
**Problem:** Not proving genuine parent-child relationship.
**Solution:**
- Provide birth certificates showing relationship
- Include family photos over time
- Show ongoing communication
- Provide evidence of visits/relationship

### Mistake 3: Inadequate Health Insurance
**Problem:** Wrong type or insufficient coverage.
**Solution:**
- Purchase Overseas Visitor Health Cover (OVHC)
- Ensure coverage for full visa duration
- Check policy meets DHA requirements
- Provide policy documentation

### Mistake 4: Applying Onshore
**Problem:** Parent in Australia when applying.
**Solution:**
- Must be outside Australia when applying
- Must be outside Australia when granted
- Can enter Australia after visa grant
- Don't apply while visiting on visitor visa

### Mistake 5: Sponsorship Timing
**Problem:** Sponsorship expires before visa lodged.
**Solution:**
- Sponsorship approval valid for 6 months only
- Lodge visa application within 6 months
- Monitor expiry dates
- Reapply for sponsorship if expires

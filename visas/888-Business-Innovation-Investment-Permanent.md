# Subclass 888 - Business Innovation and Investment (Permanent) Visa

## Section 1: Executive Overview

The Business Innovation and Investment (Permanent) visa (subclass 888) is the permanent residency pathway for holders of the Subclass 188 visa. It allows successful business owners, investors, and entrepreneurs to live in Australia permanently after meeting provisional visa obligations.

**Key Features:**
- **Visa Type:** Permanent Resident
- **Prerequisite:** Must hold Subclass 188 visa
- **Residence Requirement:** Varies by stream
- **Business/Investment Requirements:** Meet 188 stream obligations
- **Cost:** AUD $3,855 (base application charge)
- **Processing:** Variable, typically 9-18 months

**Benefits of Subclass 888:**
- Permanent residency status
- Live and work in Australia indefinitely
- Medicare access
- Pathway to citizenship
- Sponsor eligible relatives
- 5-year travel facility

## Section 2: Eligibility Requirements

### Stream-Specific Requirements

**Business Innovation Stream (888A):**
- Hold 188A visa for at least 3 years
- Reside in Australia for at least 2 years (accumulated)
- Maintain active management of Australian business
- Meet 2 of 3 business criteria:
  1. Employ 2+ Australian employees
  2. Business turnover AUD $300,000+
  3. Net personal/business assets AUD $200,000+

**Investor Stream (888B):**
- Hold 188B visa for at least 4 years
- Maintain designated investment for 4 years
- Reside in Australia for at least 2 years (accumulated)

**Significant Investor Stream (888C):**
- Hold 188C visa for at least:
  - 3 years if applied before July 1, 2015
  - 4 years if applied on/after July 1, 2015
- Maintain complying investment
- Reside in Australia for at least 40 days per year (or spouse 180 days)

**Entrepreneur Stream (888E):**
- Hold 188E visa
- Meet entrepreneurship requirements
- Successful entrepreneurial activity in Australia

### General Requirements

1. **Compliance with 188 Visa**
   - Must have complied with all 188 visa conditions
   - No visa breaches
   - Maintained business/investment as required

2. **Nomination**
   - Must maintain state/territory nomination
   - Or have completed release form from original nominating state
   - New state can nominate for 888

3. **Health and Character**
   - Meet health requirements
   - Meet character requirements
   - Family members must also meet requirements

4. **Location**
   - Can be in or outside Australia when applying
   - Must be in Australia when visa is decided (some streams)

## Section 3: Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Verify Eligibility
- Confirm 188 visa held for required period
- Calculate residence period
- Verify business/investment compliance
- Check nomination status

### Step 2: Gather Evidence
- Business financial records
- Employment records
- Residence evidence
- Investment compliance evidence
- Tax returns

### Step 3: Obtain Nomination (or Release)
- Apply for 888 nomination from state/territory
- Or obtain release from original nominating state
- State nomination requirements vary

### Step 4: Create ImmiAccount
- Register at https://online.immi.gov.au

### Step 5: Complete Application
- Select appropriate stream
- Answer all questions
- Upload supporting documents

### Step 6: Pay Application Fee
- Base charge: AUD $3,855
- Additional charges for family members

### Step 7: Submit Application
- Review carefully
- Submit application

### Step 8: Wait for Decision
- Monitor ImmiAccount
- Respond to requests
- Processing typically 9-18 months

## Section 8: Common Mistakes & Tips

### Common Mistakes

1. **Residence Miscalculation**
   - Mistake: Not meeting accumulated residence days
   - Solution: Track days carefully, keep detailed records

2. **Business Non-Compliance**
   - Mistake: Not maintaining active management
   - Solution: Document involvement continuously

3. **Missing Employees**
   - Mistake: Not meeting 2 employee requirement
   - Solution: Ensure continuous employment of 2+ people

4. **State Nomination Issues**
   - Mistake: Not obtaining required nomination
   - Solution: Apply for nomination well in advance

### Expert Tips

**Before Applying:**
- Engage qualified migration agent
- Organize business records from day one
- Track residence days carefully
- Maintain state relationship

**During Application:**
- Provide comprehensive evidence
- Ensure business financials are audited
- Document employee compliance
- Keep state nomination valid

**After Application:**
- Continue meeting requirements
- Respond promptly to requests
- Maintain valid visa status

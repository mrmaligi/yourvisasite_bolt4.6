# Subclass 190 - Skilled Nominated Visa

## Overview
The Skilled Nominated Visa (Subclass 190) is a permanent residency visa for skilled workers nominated by an Australian state or territory government.

## Processing Time
- **25% of applications:** 5 months
- **50% of applications:** 8 months
- **75% of applications:** 10 months
- **90% of applications:** 12 months

## Cost
- **Primary applicant:** $4,640 AUD
- **Additional applicant (18+):** $2,320 AUD
- **Additional applicant (under 18):** $1,160 AUD

## Key Requirements
- Be nominated by an Australian state or territory government
- Be invited to apply through SkillSelect
- Be under 45 years of age
- Have an occupation on the relevant skilled occupation list
- Have a suitable skills assessment
- Score at least 65 points (including 5 bonus points for nomination)
- Have Competent English
- Commit to living in nominating state for 2 years

## Duration
Permanent residency

## Benefits
- Permanent residency from day one
- Additional 5 points for state nomination
- Live and work in Australia permanently
- Include family members
- Access to Medicare
- Pathway to citizenship

# Subclass 402 - Training and Research Visa

> **VISA STATUS:** CLOSED (19 November 2016)
> > **Last Updated:** March 2025

---

## 1. Executive Overview

The **Training and Research visa (subclass 402)** was a temporary visa designed for individuals who wanted to come to Australia to participate in occupational training, observe or participate in research as a visiting academic, or participate in a professional development program.

### Closure Notice
- **Closed to new applications:** 19 November 2016
- **Replaced by:** Subclass 407 (Training) and Subclass 408 (Temporary Activity)

### Key Features (Historical)
- **Duration:** Up to 2 years (varied by stream)
- **Streams:** Occupational Training, Research, Professional Development
- **Work Rights:** Limited to specified activities
- **Family:** Family members could be included

### Historical Streams
1. **Occupational Training Stream** - Workplace-based training to improve skills
2. **Research Stream** - Observe or participate in Australian research projects
3. **Professional Development Stream** - Professional development programs for professionals, managers, or government officials

---

## 2. Eligibility Requirements (Historical)

### General Requirements
- **Sponsorship:** Required from approved sponsor
- **Nomination:** Required for specific activity
- **Health Insurance:** Must maintain adequate health insurance
- **Health & Character:** Meet standard requirements
- **Temporary Intent:** Genuine intention to stay temporarily

### Stream-Specific Requirements

#### Occupational Training Stream
- Appropriate qualifications and/or experience for training
- Training must be structured and supervised
- Must be genuinely for training purposes

#### Research Stream
- Invitation to observe or participate in research project
- Appropriate academic background
- Research must be conducted at Australian institution

#### Professional Development Stream
- Currently employed outside Australia
- Professional, manager, or government official
- Program must enhance professional skills

### Sponsor Requirements
- Australian organisations operating lawfully
- Foreign government agencies with Australian presence
- Must meet sponsorship obligations

## 3. Step-by-Step Process (Historical Reference)

### Step 1: Secure Sponsorship
- Find approved sponsor or have sponsor apply for approval
- Sponsor submits sponsorship application

### Step 2: Nomination
- Sponsor nominates applicant for specific stream and activity
- Submit detailed activity description

### Step 3: Visa Application
- Complete paper application (Form 1402)
- Pay visa application charge
- Submit supporting documents

### Step 4: Health and Character
- Complete medical examinations
- Obtain police clearances

### Step 5: Decision
- Wait for processing
- Receive visa grant

## 8. Common Mistakes & Tips

### Historical Mistakes:
1. **Confusion with other visas** - Many applied for wrong visa type
2. **Inadequate sponsor preparation** - Sponsor not approved before application
3. **Insufficient activity documentation** - Vague training/research plans
4. **Regular employment disguised** - Training must be genuine, not employment

### Current Alternatives (2025):
Since the 402 closed, consider these alternatives:

#### For Occupational Training:
- **Subclass 407 (Training)** - Direct replacement
- Similar requirements and structure
- Up to 2 years duration

#### For Research:
- **Subclass 408 (Temporary Activity - Research)**
- For academics and researchers
- Requires invitation from Australian institution

#### For Professional Development:
- **Subclass 407 (Training)** - Professional development stream
- **Subclass 408 (Temporary Activity)** - May apply depending on nature

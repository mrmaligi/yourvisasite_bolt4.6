# Subclass 771 - Transit Visa

## Section 1: Executive Overview

**Visa Type:** Temporary Transit Visa  
**Purpose:** Allows travelers to transit through Australia for up to 72 hours  
**Duration:** Up to 72 hours (3 days)  
**Cost:** Free  
**Processing Time:** 14-32 days

### What is the Subclass 771 Transit Visa?

The Subclass 771 Transit Visa is a **free temporary visa** that allows travelers to pass through Australia on their way to another country. This visa is designed for those who need to stop in Australia for up to 72 hours before continuing their journey to a final destination.

### Key Benefits

- **Free of charge** - No application fee
- **Quick processing** - Typically 2-4 weeks
- **Multiple entries** - Can transit multiple times on same visa (if validity permits)
- **Short stay permitted** - Up to 72 hours in Australia
- **Simple requirements** - Less documentation than other visas
- **Can leave airport** - Explore city during transit (if time permits)

### Important Limitations

- **Maximum 72 hours** - Must leave within 3 days
- **Transit purpose only** - Cannot extend or change to other visa types
- **No work permitted** - Cannot engage in employment
- **No study permitted** - Cannot enroll in courses
- **Must have onward ticket** - Proof of travel to final destination required

### Who Is This Visa For?

- Travelers transiting through Australia to another country
- Those with connecting flights requiring overnight stay
- Passengers on cruise ships stopping in Australian ports
- Travelers needing to change airports in Australia

## Section 2: Eligibility Requirements

### Core Eligibility Criteria

#### 1. Transit Purpose
- Must be **genuinely transiting** through Australia
- Must have confirmed travel to a final destination outside Australia
- Must not intend to remain in Australia beyond 72 hours
- Must have a valid reason for transit (flight connection, cruise stop, etc.)

#### 2. Onward Travel Documentation
Must provide evidence of:
- **Confirmed booking** to final destination
- Travel within 72 hours of arrival in Australia
- Valid visa for final destination (if required)
- Return/onward ticket showing departure from Australia

#### 3. Valid Passport
- Must hold a **valid passport**
- Passport must be valid for duration of stay
- Some nationalities may need additional validity beyond stay

#### 4. Character Requirements
- Must meet Australia's character requirements
- No criminal record that would prevent entry
- Not a security risk to Australia

### Special Considerations

#### Who Needs This Visa?

**Must apply for Transit Visa:**
- Citizens of certain countries (check DHA website)
- Those without eligible passport for ETA/eVisitor
- Travelers staying more than 8 hours in Australia
- Those leaving the airport transit area

**May Not Need This Visa:**
- New Zealand citizens (Special Category Visa on arrival)
- Certain passport holders eligible for ETA/eVisitor
- Those staying in airport transit area less than 8 hours

#### Transit Scenarios

**Airport Transit:**
- Changing flights at Australian airport
- Less than 8 hours: May not need visa (check eligibility)
- More than 8 hours: Usually need transit visa

**Cruise Ship Transit:**
- Stopping at Australian port
- Generally need transit visa
- Duration of stay at port counts toward 72 hours

**Multiple Transits:**
- Can use same visa for multiple transits if validity permits
- Must still comply with 72-hour limit each time

- Complete online application form

- Present visa grant notice at check-in
- Show onward ticket to airline

## Section 7: Key Considerations

### Visa Conditions

The Subclass 771 visa includes conditions:
- **Condition 8101:** No work
- **Condition 8201:** Maximum 3 months study (not practical for 72-hour visa)
- **Condition 8503:** Must not remain in Australia beyond 72 hours
- **Condition 8527:** Must maintain adequate arrangements for support

### Transit Without Visa (TWOV)

**Some travelers may not need a transit visa if:**
- Staying in airport transit area
- Transit time less than 8 hours
- Holding certain passports
- On same airline with through-checked baggage

**Check eligibility** on DHA website before applying.

### Leaving the Airport

With a Transit Visa (771), you can:
- Leave the airport transit area
- Enter Australia for up to 72 hours
- Stay in hotel or accommodation
- Tour the city briefly

You cannot:
- Work
- Study
- Extend your stay beyond 72 hours
- Apply for another visa onshore

### Connecting Flights

**Same Airport:**
- Collect baggage and re-check
- Pass through immigration and customs
- Re-enter for onward flight

**Different Airports:**
- Must travel between airports
- Consider transfer time
- Include in 72-hour calculation

## Section 8: Common Mistakes & How to Avoid Them

### Mistake 1: Applying Too Late
**Problem:** Not allowing enough processing time before travel.
**Solution:**
- Apply at least 1 month before travel
- Processing can take up to 4 weeks
- Don't book non-refundable flights until visa granted
- Apply as soon as travel confirmed

### Mistake 2: Incomplete Travel Documentation
**Problem:** Not showing clear onward travel within 72 hours.
**Solution:**
- Provide complete flight itinerary
- Show confirmed booking to final destination
- Ensure departure is within 72 hours of arrival
- Include all flight segments

### Mistake 3: Wrong Visa Type
**Problem:** Applying for transit visa when ETA/eVisitor would work.
**Solution:**
- Check if eligible for ETA (subclass 601)
- Check if eligible for eVisitor (subclass 651)
- These may be faster options
- Use DHA visa finder tool

### Mistake 4: Overstaying Transit Period
**Problem:** Booking flights that exceed 72-hour limit.
**Solution:**
- Calculate exact transit time
- Ensure departure within 72 hours
- Consider time zones
- Include buffer for delays

### Mistake 5: Not Checking Transit Without Visa Eligibility
**Problem:** Applying for visa when not needed.
**Solution:**
- Check TWOV eligibility first
- Review DHA guidance
- Check airline requirements
- Save time and effort if not needed

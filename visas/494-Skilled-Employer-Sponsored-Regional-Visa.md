# Subclass 494 - Skilled Employer Sponsored Regional (Provisional) Visa

## Section 1: Executive Overview

**Visa Type:** Provisional Work Visa (5 years)  
**Purpose:** Allows skilled workers to live and work in regional Australia with a pathway to permanent residency  
**Duration:** 5 years (pathway to PR via 191 after 3 years)  
**Cost:** AUD $4,640 (base application charge)  
**Processing Time:** 7-12 months (90% of applications)

### What is the Subclass 494 Visa?

The Subclass 494 visa is a **5-year provisional work visa** designed to help regional Australian employers fill skilled labour shortages. It replaced the Subclass 187 (RSMS) visa and provides a **direct pathway to permanent residency** through the Subclass 191 visa after 3 years of regional work.

### Key Benefits

- **5-year provisional visa** - Extended time to establish yourself in regional Australia
- **Pathway to permanent residency** - Eligible for Subclass 191 after 3 years
- **Work rights** - Work for your sponsoring employer in nominated occupation
- **Study rights** - Study in Australia (no government assistance)
- **Medicare access** - Limited access to Australia's public healthcare
- **Family inclusion** - Include partner and dependent children
- **Travel flexibility** - Travel to and from Australia for 5 years

### Who Is This Visa For?

- Skilled workers with an occupation on the relevant skilled occupation list
- Those with a job offer from a regional Australian employer
- Applicants with at least 3 years of relevant work experience
- Those willing to commit to living and working in regional Australia
- Applicants seeking a pathway to permanent residency

## Section 2: Eligibility Requirements

### Core Eligibility Criteria

#### 1. Age Requirement
- Must be **under 45 years** of age at time of application
- Exemptions may apply for certain occupations and high-income earners

#### 2. Skills Assessment
- Must have a **valid skills assessment** in your nominated occupation
- Skills assessment must be from the relevant assessing authority
- Assessment must be current at time of application

#### 3. Work Experience
- Must have at least **3 years of full-time relevant work experience**
- Experience must be at a skilled level (post-qualification)
- Can include experience gained overseas

#### 4. English Language
- Must demonstrate **competent English**
- Minimum IELTS 6.0 in each band (or equivalent)
- Acceptable tests: IELTS, PTE Academic, TOEFL iBT, Cambridge

#### 5. Occupation
- Must have an occupation on the **Skilled Occupation List** for 494
- Occupation must be eligible for the stream you're applying under
- Labour Agreement stream has different occupation requirements

#### 6. Employer Nomination
- Must be **nominated by an approved employer** in regional Australia
- Employer must be approved as a Standard Business Sponsor
- Position must be genuine and full-time

#### 7. Salary Requirement
- Must be paid at least the **Skilled Visa Income Threshold (CSIT)**
- Current threshold: **$76,515** per annum (2025)
- May be lower under Labour Agreement stream

### Regional Areas

You must work in a **designated regional area**, which includes:
- All of Australia **except** Sydney, Melbourne, and Brisbane
- Specific postcodes apply - verify with DHA

## Section 3: Step-by-Step Application Process

### Step 1: Find a Regional Employer (Variable Timeline)
- Secure a job offer from an approved regional employer
- Employer must be willing to sponsor you
- Position must be on the skilled occupation list

### Step 2: Employer Applies for Nomination (4-8 weeks)
- Employer submits nomination application
- Must demonstrate genuine need for the position
- Must meet salary and employment conditions requirements
- Employer pays nomination fee (AUD $330)

### Step 3: Gather Documents (Weeks 1-2)
- Skills assessment (if not already obtained)
- English test results
- Employment references and contracts
- Identity and character documents
- Qualification certificates

### Step 4: Create ImmiAccount (Week 2)
- Create or access your ImmiAccount
- Prepare for visa application submission

### Step 5: Lodge Visa Application (Week 3)
- Complete online application form
- Pay visa application charge (AUD $4,640)
- Upload all supporting documents
- Submit application

### Step 6: Health and Character (Weeks 3-6)
- Complete health examinations through Bupa
- Provide police clearances as requested
- Biometrics collection if required

### Step 7: Wait for Processing (Months 3-12)
- Monitor ImmiAccount for updates
- Respond promptly to any requests
- Processing typically 7-12 months

### Step 8: Visa Grant
- Receive grant notification
- Review visa conditions carefully
- Begin planning your move to regional Australia

## Section 7: Key Considerations

### Visa Conditions

When granted, your 494 visa will have conditions including:
- **Condition 8578:** Must live and work in designated regional area
- **Condition 8579:** Must work only in nominated occupation
- **Condition 8580:** Must notify DHA of address changes within 14 days
- **Condition 8581:** Must respond to DHA requests for information
- **Condition 8582:** Must work for sponsoring employer

### Pathway to Permanent Residency (191 Visa)

After holding 494 visa for 3 years:
1. Must have lived and worked in regional Australia
2. Must demonstrate 3 years of qualifying taxable income
3. Can apply for Subclass 191 (Permanent Residence)
4. No nomination or skills assessment required for 191

### Occupation Lists

The 494 visa uses the following occupation lists:
- **Medium and Long-term Strategic Skills List (MLTSSL)**
- **Regional Occupation List (ROL)**
- Check current lists on DHA website

## Section 8: Common Mistakes & How to Avoid Them

### Mistake 1: Insufficient Work Experience
**Problem:** Not meeting the 3-year experience requirement.
**Solution:**
- Count only post-qualification experience
- Ensure experience is at skilled level
- Gather strong reference letters

### Mistake 2: Invalid Skills Assessment
**Problem:** Skills assessment expired or not from correct authority.
**Solution:**
- Check assessment validity period
- Use the correct assessing authority for your occupation
- Renew if necessary before applying

### Mistake 3: Salary Below Threshold
**Problem:** Offered salary below CSIT ($76,515).
**Solution:**
- Negotiate salary before nomination
- Ensure employment contract reflects required salary
- Check current threshold (updated annually)

### Mistake 4: English Test Issues
**Problem:** Test results not meeting requirements or expired.
**Solution:**
- Ensure minimum 6.0 in each IELTS band
- Take test no more than 3 years before application
- Consider PTE Academic as alternative

### Mistake 5: Wrong Regional Area
**Problem:** Employer not in designated regional area.
**Solution:**
- Verify employer location using DHA postcode tool
- Sydney, Melbourne, and Brisbane are NOT regional
- Confirm regional status before accepting offer

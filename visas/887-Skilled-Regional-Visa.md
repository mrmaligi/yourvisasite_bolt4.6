# Subclass 887 - Skilled Regional Visa (Permanent)

## Section 1: Executive Overview

The Skilled Regional visa (subclass 887) is a permanent residence visa for people who have lived and worked in specified areas of regional Australia on an eligible provisional visa. This visa provides a pathway to permanent residency for holders of skilled provisional visas.

**Key Features:**
- **Visa Type:** Permanent Resident
- **Location Requirement:** Must be in Australia when applying
- **Residence Requirement:** 2 years in regional Australia
- **Work Requirement:** 12 months full-time work in regional Australia
- **Cost:** AUD $475 (base application charge)
- **Processing:** 17-22 months

**Benefits of Subclass 887:**
- Permanent residency status
- Live and work anywhere in Australia (after grant)
- Medicare access
- Pathway to Australian citizenship
- Sponsor eligible relatives
- 5-year travel facility

## Section 2: Eligibility Requirements

### Visa Pathway Requirements

You must have held one of these eligible visas for at least 2 years:

| Eligible Visa | Description |
|---------------|-------------|
| Subclass 489 | Skilled Regional (Provisional) - closed |
| Subclass 491 | Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) |
| Subclass 495 | Skilled Independent Regional - closed |
| Subclass 487 | Skilled Regional Sponsored - closed |

**Or:**
- Bridging visa A or B granted in relation to an application for 487, 489, or 495

### Residence Requirements

1. **Regional Residence**
   - Lived in a specified regional area of Australia for at least 2 years
   - Specified areas include most of Australia outside Sydney, Melbourne, and Brisbane
   - Check eligible postcodes on DHA website

2. **Work Requirements**
   - Worked full-time in a specified regional area for at least 12 months
   - Full-time = 35+ hours per week
   - Can be with one or multiple employers
   - Can be any type of work (does not need to be skilled)

### Additional Requirements

1. **Location at Application**
   - Must be in Australia when applying
   - Must be in Australia when visa is decided

2. **Compliance with Previous Visa**
   - Must have complied with all conditions of previous visa
   - No visa breaches or cancellations

3. **Health and Character**
   - Must meet health requirements
   - Must meet character requirements
   - Family members included must also meet requirements

### Regional Areas Definition

**Specified Regional Areas include:**
- All of Australia except: Greater Sydney, Greater Melbourne, Greater Brisbane, Gold Coast, Perth, Newcastle, Wollongong

**Postcode check:** https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/working-in-australia/regional-migration

## Section 3: Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Verify Eligibility
- Confirm eligible previous visa held
- Calculate residence period (2 years)
- Calculate work period (12 months)
- Verify regional area eligibility

### Step 2: Gather Evidence
- Residence evidence (leases, bills, bank statements)
- Employment evidence (payslips, contracts, tax records)
- Identity documents
- Health and character documents

### Step 3: Create ImmiAccount
- Register at https://online.immi.gov.au
- Verify email address

### Step 4: Complete Application
- Select "New Application"
- Choose "Skilled" → "Skilled Regional (887)"
- Answer all questions accurately

### Step 5: Upload Documents
- Evidence of residence in regional area
- Evidence of employment
- Identity documents
- Health examination (if required)
- Police certificates (if required)

### Step 6: Pay Application Fee
- Base charge: AUD $475
- Additional applicant charges apply

### Step 7: Submit Application
- Review all information
- Submit application
- Note reference number

### Step 8: Wait for Decision
- Monitor ImmiAccount
- Respond to any requests
- Processing typically 17-22 months

### Step 9: Receive Visa Grant
- Permanent residency granted
- Check visa conditions

## Section 8: Common Mistakes & Tips

### Common Mistakes

1. **Insufficient Evidence**
   - Mistake: Poor record keeping of residence/work
   - Solution: Keep meticulous records from day one

2. **Wrong Regional Area**
   - Mistake: Living/working in non-eligible postcode
   - Solution: Verify postcode eligibility before moving

3. **Part-Time Work Calculation**
   - Mistake: Not meeting 35 hours/week requirement
   - Solution: Track hours carefully, can combine multiple jobs

4. **Applying Offshore**
   - Mistake: Being outside Australia at application
   - Solution: Must be in Australia to apply

### Expert Tips

**Before Applying:**
- Start collecting evidence from day one of regional stay
- Maintain detailed work diary
- Keep all official correspondence

**During Application:**
- Organize documents chronologically
- Provide clear summaries
- Include all family members in initial application

**After Application:**
- Continue meeting requirements until decision
- Update DHA of any changes
- Maintain bridging visa conditions if applicable

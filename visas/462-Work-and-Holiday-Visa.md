# Subclass 462 - Work and Holiday Visa

## Section 1: Executive Overview

The Work and Holiday visa (subclass 462) is Australia's second Working Holiday Maker (WHM) visa option, designed for young adults from countries not eligible for the 417 visa. This visa allows recipients to holiday in Australia while undertaking short-term work and study.

**Key Features:**
- **Duration:** 12 months initially
- **Age Limit:** 18-30 years (inclusive)
- **Annual Cap:** Country-specific quotas (ballot system for some countries)
- **Work Rights:** Work for any employer up to 6 months each
- **Study Rights:** Up to 4 months study permitted
- **Cost:** AUD $635 (base application charge)
- **Processing:** Typically 20-40 days

**Second & Third Year Extensions:**
- Available after completing specified work in regional Australia
- Same 3 months (2nd year) / 6 months (3rd year) requirements as 417

**2025 Update:**
Pre-application ballot system implemented for high-demand countries including China, Vietnam, and India.

### General Eligibility Criteria

1. **Age Requirements**
   - Must be 18-30 years old at time of application
   - Must not have turned 31 before application is decided

2. **Education Requirements** (for most countries)
   - Completion of at least two years of undergraduate university education
   - Or equivalent tertiary qualification
   - Specific requirements vary by country

3. **English Language Requirements** (for some countries)
   - Functional English level
   - IELTS, TOEFL, PTE, or equivalent test scores
   - Or passport from English-speaking country
   - Or 5 years study in English

4. **Financial Requirements**
   - Sufficient funds to support yourself initially
   - Generally AUD $5,000 recommended
   - Plus funds for return/onward travel

5. **Health Requirements**
   - Meet health standards
   - May require health examination
   - Must not have tuberculosis

6. **Character Requirements**
   - No serious criminal history
   - May require police certificates

7. **Government Support Letter** (for some countries)
   - Letter of support from home country government
   - Required for countries including China, India, Vietnam

8. **Previous WHM History**
   - Must not have previously entered Australia on a 462 visa (unless applying for 2nd/3rd year)
   - Must not have held a 417 visa before

### Ballot System (2025 Update)

For high-demand countries, applicants must:
1. Register in the pre-application ballot
2. Pay AUD $25 registration fee
3. Wait for random selection
4. If selected, apply within specified timeframe

**Countries requiring ballot (2025):**
- China
- Vietnam
- India
- Bangladesh
- Indonesia (if cap reached)

## Section 3: Step-by-Step Process

### For Non-Ballot Countries:

**Step 1:** Check eligibility requirements for your country
**Step 2:** Gather required documents (education, English, funds)
**Step 3:** Create ImmiAccount
**Step 4:** Complete online application
**Step 5:** Pay application fee (AUD $635)
**Step 6:** Submit application
**Step 7:** Complete biometrics if required
**Step 8:** Wait for decision

### For Ballot Countries:

**Step 1:** Register for ballot during registration period
**Step 2:** Pay ballot registration fee (AUD $25)
**Step 3:** Wait for ballot results
**Step 4:** If selected, receive invitation to apply
**Step 5:** Complete application within specified timeframe (usually 28 days)
**Step 6:** Pay visa application fee
**Step 7:** Submit complete application
**Step 8:** Wait for decision

## Section 8: Common Mistakes & Tips

### Common Mistakes

1. **Ballot Timing**
   - Mistake: Missing ballot registration period
   - Solution: Monitor DHA website for ballot dates

2. **Education Requirements**
   - Mistake: Insufficient education documentation
   - Solution: Provide transcripts showing 2+ years

3. **English Test Validity**
   - Mistake: Expired test results
   - Solution: Ensure test taken within validity period

4. **Employer Restrictions**
   - Mistake: Working more than 6 months with one employer
   - Solution: Track employment dates carefully

### Expert Tips

- Apply for ballot as early as possible
- Prepare documents before ballot selection
- Consider 417 alternatives if eligible
- Keep detailed records for 2nd/3rd year

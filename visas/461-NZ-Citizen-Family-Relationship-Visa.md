# Subclass 461 - NZ Citizen Family Relationship Visa

## 1. Executive Overview

The **New Zealand Citizen Family Relationship (Temporary) Visa (Subclass 461)** is designed for family members of New Zealand citizens who are living in Australia on a Special Category Visa (Subclass 444). This visa allows non-NZ citizen family members to live, work, and study in Australia temporarily.

**Key Features:**
- Duration: Up to 5 years from date of grant
- Work Rights: Full work rights
- Study Rights: Full study rights
- Travel Rights: Multiple entries
- Cost: AUD $445 (2025 rate)
- Pathway: Can apply for permanent residency after 2 years in Australia

---

## 2. Eligibility Requirements

### Primary Requirements:
- **Relationship:** Must be a member of the family unit of a New Zealand citizen
- **Sponsor Status:** NZ citizen sponsor must hold a Special Category Visa (subclass 444) or be travelling to Australia to obtain one
- **Health & Character:** Must meet health and character requirements
- **Debts:** No outstanding debts to Australian Government (or repayment arrangements in place)
- **Previous Visas:** Must not have had a visa cancelled or previous application refused

### Eligible Family Members Include:
- Spouse/de facto partner
- Dependent children
- Other dependent relatives (in certain circumstances)

### Sponsor Requirements:
- Must be a New Zealand citizen
- Must hold or be eligible for Subclass 444 visa
- Must be living in Australia (or travelling to Australia)
- Must meet Australian residency requirements for PR pathway

---

## 3. Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Confirm Eligibility
- Verify your relationship to NZ citizen
- Confirm sponsor holds valid SCV (444)
- Check health and character requirements

### Step 2: Gather Documents
- Identity documents
- Relationship evidence
- Sponsor's documents
- Health and character documents
- Financial evidence

### Step 3: Complete Application
- Complete Form 147 (Application for New Zealand Citizen Family Relationship Visa)
- Complete Form 80 (Personal particulars for assessment)
- Complete Form 80 for NZ sponsor

### Step 4: Pay Fees
- Visa application charge: AUD $445
- Additional applicant fees if applicable

### Step 5: Lodge Application
- Can lodge onshore or offshore
- Can lodge by post or in person
- Some locations accept online submission

### Step 6: Health Examinations
- Complete health checks
- Provide biometrics if requested

### Step 7: Wait for Decision
- Monitor application status
- Respond to requests promptly

### Step 8: After Grant
- Can apply for permanent residency after 2 years in Australia
- Must meet residence requirements (2 years in last 5 years)

## 8. Common Mistakes & Tips

### Common Mistakes:
1. **Insufficient Relationship Evidence** - Must prove genuine relationship
2. **Sponsor Not Eligible** - Sponsor must hold valid SCV
3. **Missing Police Clearances** - Required from all relevant countries
4. **Incomplete Forms** - Form 80 must be thorough
5. **Not Understanding Conditions** - Visa has specific conditions attached
6. **Missing Health Insurance** - Consider OVHC as not eligible for Medicare

### Pro Tips:
1. **Strong Relationship Evidence** - Provide comprehensive proof of relationship
2. **Sponsor Documentation** - Ensure sponsor's SCV status is valid
3. **Financial Evidence** - Show capacity to support yourself
4. **Health Insurance** - Consider private health cover
5. **PR Pathway Planning** - Keep records for eventual permanent residency application
6. **Residence Records** - Document your time in Australia carefully

### 10G. PR Pathway Requirements

After holding 461 visa:
- Must live in Australia for 2 years
- Must hold 461 for total of 2 years in last 5 years
- Can apply for subclass 189 (NZ stream) or other PR pathways
- Must meet health and character requirements

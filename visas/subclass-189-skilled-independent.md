# Subclass 189 - Skilled Independent Visa

## Overview
The Skilled Independent Visa (Subclass 189) is a permanent residency visa for skilled workers who are not sponsored by an employer, family member, or state/territory government.

## Processing Time
- **25% of applications:** 4 months
- **50% of applications:** 6 months
- **75% of applications:** 9 months
- **90% of applications:** 12 months

## Cost
- **Primary applicant:** $4,640 AUD
- **Additional applicant (18+):** $2,320 AUD
- **Additional applicant (under 18):** $1,160 AUD

## Key Requirements
- Be invited to apply through SkillSelect
- Be under 45 years of age
- Have an occupation on the relevant skilled occupation list
- Have a suitable skills assessment for that occupation
- Score at least 65 points on the points test
- Have Competent English (IELTS 6.0 or equivalent)
- Meet health and character requirements

## Duration
Permanent residency from day one

## Benefits
- Live and work anywhere in Australia permanently
- Include family members in application
- Access to Medicare (Australia's public healthcare)
- Pathway to Australian citizenship
- Sponsor eligible relatives for permanent residence

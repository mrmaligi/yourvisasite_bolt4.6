# Subclass 408 - Temporary Activity Visa

> **VISA STATUS:** CURRENTLY OPEN
> > **Last Updated:** March 2025

---

## 1. Executive Overview

The **Temporary Activity visa (subclass 408)** allows individuals to come to Australia to participate in specific types of work or activities on a short-term, temporary basis. This is a versatile visa with multiple streams catering to different purposes.

### Key Features
- **Duration:** Up to 4 years (varies by stream)
- **Work Rights:** Depends on the specific stream
- **Family:** Family members can be included in most streams
- **Multiple Entry:** Generally permitted
- **Onshore/Offshore:** Can apply from inside or outside Australia

### Available Streams
1. **Australian Government Endorsed Events**
2. **Special Program**
3. **Religious Work**
4. **Research Activities**
5. **Staff Exchange**
6. **Sporting Activities**
7. **Entertainment Activities**
8. **Superyacht Crew**
9. **Domestic Work for Executives**
10. **Invited Participant**

---

## 2. Eligibility Requirements

### General Requirements (All Streams)
- **Sponsorship/Nomination:** Generally required from approved sponsor
- **Genuine Temporary Entrant:** Must genuinely intend to stay temporarily
- **Health Insurance:** Must maintain adequate health insurance (OVHC)
- **Health & Character:** Meet standard requirements
- **Financial Support:** Must have access to sufficient funds

### Stream-Specific Requirements

#### Australian Government Endorsed Events
- Invitation or registration for specific government-endorsed event
- Event must be listed as endorsed

#### Special Program
- Participation in government-approved special program
- Typically youth exchange, cultural, or community programs

#### Religious Work
- Full-time religious work for recognised religious institution
- Institution must be ATO-recognised

#### Research Activities
- Invitation to observe or participate in research project
- Typically for academics, researchers, research students

#### Staff Exchange
- Participation in staff exchange arrangement
- Must benefit Australian and overseas parties

#### Sporting Activities
- Participation in sporting event or coaching
- Support from relevant sporting body

#### Entertainment Activities
- Performance, production, or support work in entertainment industry
- Support from industry sponsor

#### Superyacht Crew
- Work as crew member on superyacht
- Yacht must be intending to leave Australian waters

#### Domestic Work for Executives
- Full-time domestic work for senior foreign executive
- Executive must hold specific visa types

#### Invited Participant
- Invitation to participate in cultural/social activity
- Short-term visits (up to 3 months)

## 3. Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Determine Eligible Stream
- Identify which stream matches your intended activity
- Verify stream requirements

### Step 2: Secure Sponsorship/Invitation
- Obtain sponsorship from approved sponsor, OR
- Secure invitation for relevant activity
- Sponsor may need to apply for approval

### Step 3: Gather Documents
- Prepare personal documents
- Obtain health insurance
- Complete health examinations (if required)
- Obtain police clearances

### Step 4: Complete Application
- Create or log into ImmiAccount
- Select appropriate stream
- Complete online application form
- Upload supporting documents

### Step 5: Pay Fees
- Pay visa application charge
- Additional fees for family members

### Step 6: Decision
- Wait for processing
- Respond to any requests for information
- Receive visa grant

## 8. Common Mistakes & Tips

### Common Mistakes
1. **Wrong stream selection** - Choose the stream that accurately describes your activity
2. **Insufficient sponsorship evidence** - Ensure sponsor is approved and nomination valid
3. **Inadequate health insurance** - Must maintain OVHC for entire stay
4. **Unclear activity purpose** - Application must clearly demonstrate temporary intent
5. **Missing documentation** - Each stream has specific document requirements
6. **COVID-19 Pandemic Event confusion** - This stream has largely closed

### Tips for Success
1. **Select correct stream** - Read stream requirements carefully
2. **Secure sponsor early** - Processing can be delayed if sponsor not approved
3. **Maintain health insurance** - Arrange before arrival, keep current
4. **Document temporary intent** - Show strong ties to home country
5. **Provide detailed itinerary** - For event/program participation
6. **Check special program eligibility** - Some programs have specific requirements

### Important Conditions
- **Condition 8101:** No work (for some streams)
- **Condition 8107:** Work only for sponsor (for work streams)
- **Condition 8501:** Maintain health insurance
- **Condition 8534:** No further stay (may apply)

### 10C. Timeline Factors

**Fastest Processing:**
- Government endorsed events (priority processing)
- Sporting activities with clear event dates
- Entertainment with production schedules
- Countries with streamlined processing
- Complete documentation

**Slowest Processing:**
- Research Activities stream (current delays)
- Applicants from certain countries (enhanced checks)
- Complex sponsorship arrangements
- Incomplete applications
- High-volume periods

**Current Research Activities Stream Issues:**
- Significant delays for Chinese nationals (200+ applicants affected)
- Enhanced security screening implemented 2024
- Applications from August 2024 onwards experiencing delays
- Some applications from 2023 still pending

**Common Delay Reasons:**

# Subclass 601 - Electronic Travel Authority (ETA)

## Section 1: Executive Overview

The Electronic Travel Authority (subclass 601) is Australia's fastest and most convenient visa option for short-term visits. Available to passport holders from select countries, the ETA allows multiple entries to Australia over a 12-month period.

**Key Features:**
- **Duration:** 12 months from issue date
- **Stay Period:** Up to 3 months per visit
- **Cost:** AUD $20 (service fee)
- **Processing:** Usually instant to 48 hours
- **Entry:** Multiple entries permitted

**Important Update (2025):**
All ETA-eligible passport holders must now apply using the **Australian ETA app**, which requires a live facial scan. The web-based application system has been phased out.

## Section 2: Eligibility Requirements

### Passport Requirements

You must hold a valid passport from one of these eligible countries/regions:

| Region | Countries |
|--------|-----------|
| **Asia-Pacific** | Brunei, Hong Kong (SAR), Japan, Malaysia, Singapore, South Korea, Taiwan |
| **North America** | Canada, United States |
| **Europe** | Andorra, Austria, Belgium, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Iceland, Ireland, Italy, Liechtenstein, Luxembourg, Malta, Monaco, Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, San Marino, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, United Kingdom (British Citizen) |
| **Other** | Vatican City |

### General Eligibility Criteria

1. **Genuine Temporary Entrant**
   - Intention to visit Australia temporarily
   - Purpose: Tourism or business visitor activities only
   - Not for work (beyond business visitor activities)

2. **Health Requirements**
   - Must meet basic health standards
   - No medical examination typically required
   - Must not pose a public health risk

3. **Character Requirements**
   - No serious criminal record
   - No previous visa cancellations or refusals
   - Not subject to an exclusion period

4. **Other Conditions**
   - Must be outside Australia when applying
   - Must be outside Australia when the ETA is granted
   - Must not have any outstanding debts to the Australian Government

### Ineligibility Factors

You are NOT eligible for an ETA if:
- You have tuberculosis
- You have a criminal conviction with a sentence of 12 months or more
- You have previously been removed or deported from Australia
- Your passport is from a non-eligible country
- You intend to work in Australia (beyond business visitor activities)
- You intend to study for more than 3 months

## Section 3: Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Download the Australian ETA App

**For iOS:**
- Download from Apple App Store
- Search "Australian ETA"

**For Android:**
- Download from Google Play Store
- Search "Australian ETA"

### Step 2: Create an Account
- Open the app
- Enter email address
- Create a password
- Verify your email

### Step 3: Complete the Application
- Scan your passport (photo page)
- Complete live facial scan
- Answer health and character questions
- Provide contact details

### Step 4: Pay the Application Fee
- Fee: AUD $20 per applicant
- Payment via credit/debit card
- Each family member requires a separate application

### Step 5: Wait for Processing
- Most applications processed instantly
- Some may take up to 48 hours
- Notification sent via email and app

### Step 6: Receive ETA Confirmation
- ETA electronically linked to passport
- Check status in the app
- No physical visa label required

## Section 8: Common Mistakes & Tips

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Incorrect Passport Details**
   - Mistake: Typos in passport number or name
   - Solution: Triple-check all passport information

2. **Passport Validity Issues**
   - Mistake: Passport expiring soon
   - Solution: Ensure passport valid for duration of stay

3. **Using Old Application Method**
   - Mistake: Trying to apply via website
   - Solution: Must use Australian ETA app only

4. **Assuming ETA Covers All Activities**
   - Mistake: Planning to work on ETA
   - Solution: ETA only allows tourism/business visitor activities

5. **Applying While in Australia**
   - Mistake: Attempting to apply onshore
   - Solution: Must be outside Australia to apply

### Expert Tips for Success

**Before Applying:**
- Download app and test functionality before travel
- Ensure passport is in good condition for scanning
- Have good lighting for facial scan

**During Application:**
- Use strong, stable internet connection
- Follow app instructions carefully
- Complete facial scan in good lighting

**After Approval:**
- Screenshot confirmation for records
- Check ETA details match passport
- Note ETA expiry date (12 months from grant)

### Understanding ETA Conditions

**You CAN:**
- Visit Australia multiple times
- Stay up to 3 months per visit
- Engage in tourist activities
- Conduct business visitor activities
- Study for up to 3 months

**You CANNOT:**
- Work in Australia
- Stay longer than 3 months per visit
- Extend the ETA while in Australia
- Apply for another ETA while holding a current one

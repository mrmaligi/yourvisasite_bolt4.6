# Subclass 489 - Skilled Regional (Provisional) Visa

## 1. Executive Overview

**IMPORTANT: This visa is CLOSED to new applications as of 16 November 2019.**

The **Skilled Regional (Provisional) Visa (Subclass 489)** was a 4-year provisional visa for skilled workers who wanted to live and work in regional Australia. It has been replaced by the **Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) Visa (Subclass 491)**.

**Status:** 
- **Closed to new applications: 16 November 2019**
- Replaced by: Subclass 491
- Existing 489 holders can still apply for Subclass 887 (Permanent)

**Historical Key Features:**
- Duration: 4 years
- Regional Requirement: Must live in regional Australia
- Points-Tested: Yes (minimum 65 points)
- Pathway to PR: Subclass 887 after 2 years

---

## 2. Eligibility Requirements (Historical)

### Requirements When Visa Was Open:

#### Primary Requirements:
- **Age:** Under 45 years
- **Skills Assessment:** Positive skills assessment in eligible occupation
- **Points:** Minimum 65 points on skilled migration points test
- **English:** Competent English minimum
- **Nomination:** Nominated by state/territory OR sponsored by eligible relative in regional area
- **Health & Character:** Must meet requirements

#### Two Pathways:

**1. State/Territory Nominated:**
- Nominated by Australian state or territory government
- Must live and work in nominating state

**2. Family Sponsored:**
- Sponsored by eligible relative living in designated area
- Relative must be Australian citizen, permanent resident, or eligible NZ citizen
- Relative must be 18+ years

#### Designated Areas:
- All of Australia except Sydney, Melbourne, and Brisbane
- Specific postcodes applied

## 3. Step-by-Step Process (Historical)

### When Visa Was Open:

1. **Skills Assessment** - Obtain positive assessment
2. **Submit EOI** - Via SkillSelect
3. **Receive Nomination/Sponsorship** - From state or family member
4. **Invitation** - Receive invitation to apply
5. **Apply for Visa** - Within 60 days of invitation
6. **Health & Character** - Complete checks
7. **Wait for Decision** - Processing varied
8. **Move to Regional Area** - Within specified timeframe

## 10. REPLACEMENT VISA: SUBCLASS 491

### 10A. Subclass 491 Overview

The **Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) Visa (Subclass 491)** replaced the 489 visa and offers:

**Key Features:**
- Duration: 5 years (increased from 489's 4 years)
- Points: Additional 15 points for regional nomination
- Regional Requirement: Live and work in regional Australia
- PR Pathway: Subclass 191 (Permanent Residence Regional) after 3 years

### 10B. Subclass 491 Requirements

- **Age:** Under 45 years
- **Skills Assessment:** Positive assessment
- **Points:** Minimum 65 points (includes 15 points for regional nomination)
- **English:** Competent English
- **Nomination:** State/territory nomination or family sponsorship

### 10C. Comparison: 489 vs 491

| Feature | 489 (Closed) | 491 (Current) |
|---------|-------------|---------------|
| Duration | 4 years | 5 years |
| Points for Nomination | 10 points | 15 points |
| PR Pathway | Subclass 887 | Subclass 191 |
| Years to PR | 2 years | 3 years |
| Regional Definition | Designated areas | Regional postcodes |
| Status | Closed | Open |

### 10D. Regional Areas Definition

**Regional Australia includes:**
- All of Australia except Sydney, Melbourne, and Brisbane
- Specific postcodes designated as regional
- Check current regional postcodes on Home Affairs website

### 10E. Pathway to Permanent Residency

**For 489 Holders:**
- Apply for Subclass 887 after 2 years
- Must live and work in regional area

**For 491 Holders:**
- Apply for Subclass 191 after 3 years
- Must have taxable income at or above specific threshold
- Must comply with regional conditions

### 10F. Tips for Regional Migration

1. **Research Regional Areas** - Different states have different opportunities
2. **State Nomination** - Each state has different occupation lists
3. **Job Market** - Research employment in your field
4. **Cost of Living** - Often lower in regional areas
5. **Community** - Regional communities are welcoming
6. **Documentation** - Keep records of residence and employment

### 10G. Current Recommendation

Those interested in the former 489 pathway should now:

1. **Consider Subclass 491** - Direct replacement with better benefits
2. **Check State Nomination Lists** - Each state has different priorities
3. **Family Sponsorship** - If have eligible relative in regional area
4. **Points Improvement** - Higher points = better chance of invitation
5. **Regional Job Search** - Secure employment before moving

---

## 11. Summary

The Subclass 489 visa has been permanently closed and replaced by the Subclass 491 visa, which offers better terms including 5-year duration and higher points allocation.

Existing 489 visa holders should focus on meeting the requirements for the Subclass 887 permanent visa.
